Trixie Sherman

Purpose:

  • Protect others.
  • Make William Afton pay.
  • Find her lost friends.

Background:

Trixie was a strange but friendly girl who always wanted to feel noticed and appreciated. She often talked to herself and feared being forgotten or abandoned. She became close friends with several children who would later possess animatronics, and her boyfriend, Oscar, disappeared alongside them. While searching for her missing friends, Trixie also disappeared and later became one of the animatronics.

Personality:

During the Day:

  • Friendly and social.
  • Protective of others.
  • Kind-hearted and well-meaning.

During the Night:

  • Aggressive and unstable.
  • Desperate for attention.
  • Driven by anger and revenge.

Likes & Dislikes:

Likes:
  • Oscar/Chuck (boyfriend).
  • Holly/Bonabi (friend).
  • Her fellow animatronics.
Dislikes:
  • Being alone.
  • Feeling unloved.
  • Abandonment.
  • Being damaged or torn apart.
  • William Afton.

Relationships:

Oscar (Chuck): Her boyfriend. She deeply cares about him and wants to discover the truth about his death.

Holly (Bonabi): One of her closest friends. She still struggles to accept what happened to Holly and everyone else.

Notes:

• Often talks to herself, as if she has two minds.
• Has a strong need to feel loved and accepted.
• Vixen was her favorite animatronic before her death.
• Dislikes bright lights at night.
• Makes distorted, garbled sounds when angry or damaged.
